How to play

  • Collect cyan light fragments to grow longer and gain score.
  • Amber fragments are rare and give significant bonus points.
  • Avoid red corrupt shards. They will terminate the signal immediately.
  • Do not leave the arena. Wall collision will terminate the signal.
  • Move with mouse, touch, WASD or arrow keys.
  • Press SPACE to trigger a Pulse that clears nearby threats (has cooldown).
  • Survive as long as possible to reach maximum intensity.
