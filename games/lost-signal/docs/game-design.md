# Lost Signal — Game Design (POV Mode)

## Core Idea
A first-person point-and-click puzzle adventure about investigating mysterious signals originating from beneath the Taren surface.

## Player Perspective
Players experience the world directly through a first-person POV (Point-of-View). This creates a more immersive, atmospheric experience focused on environmental observation and logical deduction.

## Core Mechanics
- **POV Exploration**: Direct interaction with high-quality cinematic backgrounds.
- **Inventory System**: Collecting items and applying them to the world via the sidebar.
- **Environmental Puzzles**: Solving problems through observation and item interaction.
- **Bilingual Narrative**: Storytelling through object descriptions and system feedback in English and Swedish.

## The First Scene: The Dock
The player arrives at an old, abandoned dock outside Sector 7.
- **Primary Goal**: Restore power to the main signal station door.
- **Current Objective**: Find the missing fuse and install it.

## Design Aesthetic
- **Cinematic & Atmospheric**: Heavy focus on visual storytelling and mood.
- **Tech-Noir**: Amber and cyan lighting against dark, industrial/rocky environments.
- **Clean UI**: Minimalist sidebar that stays out of the way of the main visuals.
