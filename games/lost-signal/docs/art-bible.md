# Lost Signal — Art Bible

The visual identity of Lost Signal is "Mysterious Tech-Noir Adventure".

## Core Pillars
1. **Atmospheric Contrast**: Deep shadows contrasted with vibrant, purposeful light sources.
2. **2.5D Depth**: Using layers (background, fog, character, vignettes) to simulate depth without true 3D.
3. **Calm Mystery**: The environment should feel quiet and waiting, not aggressive or horrific.
4. **Handcrafted Tech**: Technology looks old, used, and slightly out of place in the natural environment.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Dark**: Deep Navy / Charcoal (`#0a0a12`)
- **Primary Light**: Amber (`#ffb347`) - represents life, heat, and old power.
- **Secondary Light**: Signal Cyan (`#00ffff`) - represents mysterious data, AI, and the "Signal".

## Visual Effects
- **Vignetting**: Focuses the player's attention on the center of the scene.
- **Atmospheric Fog**: Softens the background and adds a sense of air and scale.
- **Signal Glow**: Subtle pulse animations on cyan elements to indicate they are "active".

## Composition
- Fixed 16:9 camera angle.
- Slightly low-angle perspective to make environments feel larger.
- Focal points (hotspots) should be balanced within the frame.
