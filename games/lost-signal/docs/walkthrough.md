# Lost Signal — Walkthrough

Follow these steps to complete the first build:

1. Click **Start Expedition** on the start screen.
2. **Move Nilo**: Click any valid ground area to move Nilo.
3. Click the **Old Sign** on the left.
    - Nilo will walk to the sign first.
    - Once he arrives, he will find a **Rusty Fuse**.
4. Open the **Inventory** at the bottom.
5. Click the **Rusty Fuse** icon.
    - The icon will be highlighted in cyan, indicating it is selected.
6. Click the **Rusty Fuse Box** on the right side of the dock.
    - Nilo will walk to the fuse box.
    - Once he arrives, the box will pulse with cyan light.
    - Power is now restored.
7. Click the **Locked Signal Door**.
    - Nilo will walk to the door.
    - It will now show a message saying it is powered but needs a code for the next build.
