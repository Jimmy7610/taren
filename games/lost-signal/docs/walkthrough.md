# Lost Signal — Walkthrough (POV Mode)

Follow these steps to complete the dock sequence:

1. Click **Start Expedition** on the start screen.
2. **Examine Scene**: Hover over objects to see labels.
3. Click the **Old Sign** on the left.
    - You will find a **Rusty Fuse**.
    - It automatically appears in your **Inventory** in the left sidebar.
4. Click the **Rusty Fuse** icon in the sidebar.
    - The icon will be highlighted in cyan, indicating it is selected.
5. Click the **Rusty Fuse Box** on the right side of the dock.
    - The scene will pulse with light.
    - Power is now restored.
    - Your objective updates to "Explore the station interior."
6. Click the **Locked Signal Door**.
    - It will now show a message saying it is powered but needs an authorization code.
