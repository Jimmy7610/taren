How to play

  • Build Foundations from Ace to King by suit.
  • Stacks must alternate colors and be in descending order.
  • Empty columns can only hold a King.
  • Click the Stock to draw a card.
  • Click a card to select, click a pile to move.
  • R to Reset. U to Undo.
