How to play

  • Protect the glowing core.
  • Move your mouse or finger around the arena to aim the shield opening.
  • Let blue signals pass through the opening.
  • Block red signals with the solid shield wall.
  • Amber signals give bonus points.
  • Press SPACE to trigger Pulse Shield when too many signals get close.
  • Game over when core integrity reaches 0%.
