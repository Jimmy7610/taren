About Taren

Taren is a personal test project by Jimmy Eliasson — a small digital space for browser games, experiments and playful ideas.

It is vibe-coded step by step: tested, adjusted, broken, fixed and shaped as it grows. The current collection started with lightweight browser games, but Taren is not locked to one specific technology forever.

This is not meant to be a finished commercial platform. It is a living lab where ideas can start small, become playable and slowly improve over time.

If you want to get in touch, you can reach me at jbl_76@hotmail.com.
